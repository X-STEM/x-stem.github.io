What is XSTEM?

XSTEM is a club where students work together to create their own companies and compete to win prizes at the regional, national, and international level! Through this club students will learn a MIT based curriculum to develop their companies.

Our meetings take place in the DLC (3rd Floor Annex) on Tuesdays until 4:00 PM.
