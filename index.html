What is XSTEM?

XSTEM is a club where students work together to create their own companies and compete to win prizes at the regional, national, and international level! Through this club students will learn a MIT based curriculum to develop their companies and gain valuable knowledge from their mentors.

Our meetings take place in the DLC (3rd Floor Annex) on Tuesdays until 4:00 PM.

Event Details

Date: Friday, April 26th, 2025
Time: 8:50 AM - 1:00 PM
Location: Freshman Cafeteria

This is our culminating event where teams will pitch their business ideas!

Required for Club Credit

What to Bring

  • Laptop with charger
  • Team poster board
  • Final slide deck (ready to present)
  • Business professional attire
  • Any demo materials for your product

Make sure to arrive on time to set up with your team!

Dress Code

Business/Business Formal attire is required for this event.

Professional suits, blazers, dress shirts
Dress pants, skirts, blouses, dresses
Formal shoes (no sneakers)
